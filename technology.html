Our Technology

Advanced AI-powered hippocampus segmentation

CereVolum utilizes state-of-the-art deep learning algorithms to provide accurate and reliable hippocampus segmentation from structural MRI scans. Our technology has been optimized for both research and clinical applications.

The system automatically processes T1-weighted MRI images, identifies hippocampal boundaries, and provides volumetric measurements with sub-millimeter precision. All processing is performed in standardized space for consistent results across scanners and sites.

Key Features:


  • Fully automated processing pipeline
  • Left/right hippocampus segmentation
  • Volumetric analysis in mm³ and cm³
  • Quality control metrics
  • Standardized output formats

Technical Specifications

Input

MRI ( Nifti (nii.gz) and Analyze (.hdr/.img) format)

Output

Segmentation masks, volumetric measurements

Processing Time

< 10 minutes per scan

Compatibility

Windows/Linux/Mac
